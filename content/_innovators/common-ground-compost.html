Our goal is to keep edible food from going to waste, divert organic waste from landfills, and assist in the development, implementation, and growth of waste diversion programs for all. Everything we do is based in transparency and education, and we seek to empower our clients to make smart and informed decisions by understanding their waste streams. Common Ground Compost manages Reclaimed Organics, a program that collects food scraps by bicycle in New York City. Our pilot launched in the East Village of Manhattan, and has grown to accept material from the West Village, SoHo, and the Lower East Side.
