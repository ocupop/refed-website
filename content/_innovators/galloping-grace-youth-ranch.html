--- layout: innovator name: Galloping Grace Youth Ranch city: Rio Rancho state: new-mexico country: USA innovator_level: local description: >- Galloping Grace Youth Ranch, a nonprofit working ranch in Rio Rancho New Mexico, educates children about ranching, farming and sustainable practices through the raising of livestock and the growing of crops. The Ranch has working relationships with area grocery stores, food banks and schools to collect over 1 million pounds of food annually that would otherwise go to the landfill. The Ranch uses these food scraps to feed their herd of cattle, pigs, chickens and goats at the ranch. The livestock are used in youth camps, 4-H projects, and a number of youth-focused farm educational programs. When the livestock reach finishing weight, they are sent to a local processing facility and the meat is subsequently donated back to the regional food bank.
