In the U.S. we spend $218 billion a year growing, processing, transporting, and disposing of food that is never eaten. However, one of the key challenges in the field is lack of data both on the problem itself and on the most effective solutions. ReFED’s Data and Insights team uses economics and data to generate actionable solutions to reduce the amount of food that goes uneaten.
Theory of Change
Our vision is a sustainable, resilient, and inclusive food system that optimizes environmental resources, minimizes climate impacts, and makes the best use of the food we grow. Our mission to get there is to end food loss and waste across the food system by advancing data-driven solutions.
