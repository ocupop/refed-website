Rethinking food waste through economics and data — it is in our name and in our DNA. Along with hundreds of food system actors, we have been actively transforming the way we understand and work within the food system by providing the nation’s most comprehensive insights on food waste solutions. From the original 27 solutions analyzed in the Roadmap to the forthcoming new solutions set generated by the Insights Engine, ReFED serves as a trusted voice for the sector, distinctive in our aim to leverage data to promote a sustainable, resilient, and inclusive food system.
Our Work Focuses include:

  • Data & Insights

    The Data and Insights team uses economics and data to generate actionable solutions to reduce the amount of food that goes uneaten.
    Learn More

  • Capital & Innovation

    The Capital & Innovation team works with capital providers, food businesses, technology companies, innovators, and policymakers across the food system to drive the adoption of and catalyze public, private, and philanthropic funding for effective, scalable food waste solutions.
    Learn More

  • Stakeholder Engagement

    The Stakeholder Engagement team works to convene key stakeholders across private and public entities around food waste solutions and issues that require a systems-based approach.
    Learn More
