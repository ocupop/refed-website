Reducing Food Waste Through Good Policy

The Roadmap highlights a number of areas where policy can facilitate solutions that will reduce food waste by 20%. To achieve this, the immediate priority is to spread and advocate for best-practice policies at the local and state levels. Reaching greater long-term goals will likely require a comprehensive federal food waste policy that sets national guidelines.

Enacting supportive food waste reduction policies has the potential to clear up consumer confusion about date labeling, spur increased donations to food recovery e orts, incentivize more businesses to reduce food waste, and develop more robust food waste recycling infrastructure.
