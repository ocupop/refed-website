  1. For which foods does the federal government regulate date labels? Check all that apply.

    • Answer: Infant Formula

      Infant formula is the only product for which a date label is federally regulated. This is not because of a safety concern, but because the nutrients in formula decline over time. All other labeling requirements are stipulated by a patchwork of state and local laws.
