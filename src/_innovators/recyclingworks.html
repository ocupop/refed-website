--- layout: innovator name: RecyclingWorks city: Northampton state: massachusetts country: USA innovator_level: local description: >- RecyclingWorks in Massachusetts (RecyclingWorks) is a recycling assistance program designed to help businesses and institutions maximize waste diversion opportunities. RecyclingWorks provides no-cost technical assistance, guidance documents, case studies, and more to help businesses succeed in starting or expanding any waste diversion program. Working across the EPA Food Recovery Hierarchy, RecyclingWorks connects businesses to solution providers to prevent, recover, and recycle wasted food. We also work with compost operations to help ensure well-operated sites by providing consulting advice on best management practices.
