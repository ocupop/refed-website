Join Our Solution Provider Directory

The Solution Provider Directory showcases over 700 for-profits, nonprofits, and individuals with ready-to-implement food waste reduction solutions. Being a part of the Solution Provider Directory will give you access to ReFED’s growing network of food businesses, funders, subject matter experts, researchers, and government agencies. You will also receive notifications about future funding opportunities, upcoming events, and industry reports.

Join the Solution Provider Directory

Join Our Expert Network

The ReFED Expert Network is a world-class group of more than 100 experts who share their knowledge and time on key projects at ReFED, as well as connect with, teach, and learn from their Expert Network peers. Experts bring perspectives from all parts of the food system and a diverse set of backgrounds. Together, they are influencing and strengthening impactful food waste-reducing work in pursuit of our shared goal: a 50% reduction in food waste by the year 2030. If you are a food systems professional that shares our vision of promoting a sustainable, resilient, and inclusive food system, contact us to learn how you can join.
