Each year in the United States, 34.2% of food produced – 92.8 million tons – is never eaten. While this has enormous impacts on our environment, food insecurity, the economy, and more, many businesses, jurisdictions, nonprofits, funders, and others are already making a substantial impact to address the challenge. But much more needs to be done to achieve national and international goals to reduce food loss and waste by 50% by the year 2030.
