Theory of Change

Our vision is a sustainable, resilient, and inclusive food system that optimizes environmental resources, minimizes climate impacts, and makes the best use of the food we grow. Our mission to get there is to end food loss and waste across the food system by advancing data-driven solutions.

Strategies {% include icons/arrow.svg %}

Motivate More actors through compelling data, actionable guidance and strategic communications

Mobilize More Action through catalyzing capital, collaboration, and innovation

Advancing Solutions By increasing the knowledge base and setting the course for coordinated efforts

Activities {% include icons/arrow.svg %}

Actionable Data & Insights Providing food waste data and insights to guide action through the Insights Engine, Roadmap and Progress Reports

Stakeholder Engagement Convening stakeholders to share information and resources around challenges and solutions

Capital & Innovation Driving capital to solutions and supporting solution providers growth

Thought Leadership Promoting big-picture thinking to encourage evolution of the food system
