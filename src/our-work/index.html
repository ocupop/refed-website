Rethinking food waste through economics and data — it is in our name and in our DNA. Along with hundreds of food system actors, we have been actively transforming the way we understand and work within the food system by providing the nation’s most comprehensive insights on food waste solutions. From the original 27 solutions analyzed in the Roadmap to the forthcoming new solutions set generated by the Insights Engine, ReFED serves as a trusted voice for the sector, distinctive in our aim to leverage data to promote a sustainable, resilient, and inclusive food system.

Our Work Focuses include:

Data & Insights

The Data and Insights team leverages data and insights to highlight supply-chain inefficiencies and economic opportunities to generate actionable solutions to reduce the amount of food that goes uneaten.

Learn More

Capital & Innovation

The Capital & Innovation team works with capital providers, food businesses, technology companies, innovators, and policymakers across the food system to  catalyze public, private, and philanthropic capital to spur innovation and high-impact initiatives 

Learn More

Stakeholder Engagement

The Stakeholder Engagement team works to mobilize, connect, and convene  supporters across private and public entities to take targeted action  around food waste solutions and issues that require a systems-based approach.
