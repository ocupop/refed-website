Hundreds of Innovators, One Location

Explore the dynamic, expanding food waste innovation sector, broken down by food waste solution type, organizational status, and geographic location. The ReFED Food Waste Innovator Database is a living compilation of commercial and nonprofit entities turning the food waste problem into an opportunity for economic, social, and environmental impact. To get a perspective on the measureable impacts of the broader Innovator Categories, visit the ‘Related Solution(s)’ pages linked to each ‘Innovator Detail’ page.

Related food waste databases

ReFED’s Food Waste Innovator Database focuses on food waste innovation, but there are a number of complementary tools that list additional organizations tackling food waste. We encourage you to check out these valuable resources, and let us know if there is something missing!

  • ReFED's Policy Tool

    A comprehensive overview of federal and state policies that facilitate food waste reduction and diversion.

  • Food Rescue Locator

    A directory of organizations across the U.S. that rescue, glean, transport, prepare, and distribute food to food insecure populations.

  • OpenIDEO Food Waste Initiatives

    From July to October 2016, OpenIDEO ran the global “Food Waste Challenge” – now, the organization is continuing its food waste work with new initiatives.

  • EPA & USDA Further with Food

    An online hub and first-of-its-kind aggregator of existing food waste research and resources.

  • BioCycle Resources

    Searchable databases of composting facilities, anaerobic digestion facilities, and organics collection services across the U.S. and Canada.
