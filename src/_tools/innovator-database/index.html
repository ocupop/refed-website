Over 350 Innovators

The ReFED Innovator Database is a living compilation of 350+ commercial and nonprofit entities that are turning the food waste problem into an entrepreneurial opportunity. The database is currently populated as ReFED learns of new or existing innovators, though in the future, users will be able to suggest additional organizations for inclusion. Innovators are grouped by where they fall in the food recovery hierarchy (prevention, recovery, and recycling), and then placed into one of 13 Innovator Database categories.
