Suggest an Innovator

We need your help to keep a pulse on food waste innovation – what we define as changing the status quo on a national or regional scale to: prevent food from going to waste; recover safe, edible food to feed people; or capture value from food that is wasted. If you can’t find what you’re looking for, or if you see an entry that needs an update, please reach out today.

We currently have two criteria for inclusion in the database:

  • Must be in operation and actively involved in preventing, recovering, or recycling food waste. Before you reach out, ask yourself: "Is my organization active? Has my product reached market? Are my services available?"
  • We want to know why your organization should be included. Please let us know below.
