Suggest an Innovator

Although ReFED wants to highlight the full spectrum of innovative food waste organizations, we do have two pieces of criteria for inclusion. First, we are only listing entities that are in operation and actively involved in preventing, recovering, or recycling food waste. Before you reach out, ask yourself: "Is my organization active? Has my product reached market? Are my services available?" If the answer is no, come back once your idea has been developed! Second, we want to know why your organization should be included. Please let us know below. If you have any questions, don't hesitate to reach out at info@refed.com. Thanks!
