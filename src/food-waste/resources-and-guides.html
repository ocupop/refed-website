Identifying the Issue at Hand

Roadmap to 2030

Food waste is a systemwide problem, and solving it will require a systemwide response. Our Roadmap to 2030: Reducing U.S. Food Waste looks at the entire food supply chain and identifies seven key action areas to help guide the food system’s efforts over the next ten years. In line with the Target-Measure-Act framework for food waste reduction that’s been adopted around the world, the Roadmap to 2030 is a critical blueprint to move the food system to action.
